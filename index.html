The Story

I started with data.
People brought me here.

I started with a Computer Science background — good at patterns, logic, and systems. But somewhere along the way, I got more interested in a different kind of system: why people pay attention to certain things and scroll past everything else.

That curiosity led me into content. I joined StrongBox IT as an intern, learning the ropes — content calendars, audience research, finding the topics that actually resonated. When the senior manager left, I didn't step back. I stepped up. I took ownership of the entire marketing function: the team, the strategy, the SEO, the reporting, the conversations with the COO.

I speak both languages — creative and analytical. I track performance gaps in GA4, build insights from SQL and Python, and use that to shape content that connects with the right audience at the right time.

I'm now building content strategy for multiple clients as an independent consultant — and actively seeking my next full-time role with a team that takes content seriously.

Belief 01

Data tells you what. Curiosity tells you why.

Numbers show patterns. Strategy comes from asking what they mean about human behavior — and what to do about it.

Belief 02

The best content doesn't sell. It makes people feel understood.

When an audience feels like you get them, trust follows. And trust converts better than any ad ever will.

Belief 03

Strategy without empathy is just a spreadsheet.

You can have perfect data and still miss the audience entirely if you forget there are real people on the other end.

Belief 04

Consistency builds brands. Virality doesn't.

One viral moment is a spike. Showing up every week with something worth reading — that's what builds an audience you keep.
